How to update Alex River's expected probation office

! Warning It is not possible to change the probation office within this service, as a change of location may affect the eligibility of the referral.

Talk to the service provider

You must first discuss the change of probation office location with the service provider. They will let you know if they are still eligible to deliver the intervention.

Next steps

If the referral is still eligible

Update the case notes to include the new probation office location.

If the referral is no longer eligible

Cancel the referral and create a new referral with a different service provider.
