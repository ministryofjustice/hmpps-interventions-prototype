Description

Finance, Benefits and Debt (FBD) is a rehabilitative service to help men better understand banking, stabilise debt, and claim benefits.

Outcomes

By the end of the programme, participants will be able to:

  • create a monthly budget
  • manage finances independently for 3 consecutive months with no missed payments
  • create a repayment plan for 75% of their debts within 8 weeks
  • identify at least 3 financial behaviours linked to offending and develop strategies to change these
  • find and apply for all benefits and financial support they’re eligible for

Session details

Sessions are held in a group or one-to-one depending on the participant’s needs.
