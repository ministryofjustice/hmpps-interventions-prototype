Description

Maps for Change is a work-based resource for probation practitioners in the community. It provides a structured framework for supervising men convicted of sexual offences.

The toolkit has exercises designed for participants with a low risk of reoffending. Each topic covers a different criminogenic need and helps develop their abilities and resilience.

Who is eligible

To be eligible, men must:

  • have a conviction for a sexual or sexually-motivated offence
  • have a low risk of reoffending
  • have a low OASys sexual reoffending predictor (direct contact or indecent images) or a medium OASys sexual reoffending predictor if they are not eligible for an equivalent accredited programme

The programme is delivered by a practitioner in the community.

Outcomes

By the end of the programme, participants will:

  • identify 3 personal risk factors through self-assessment
  • develop relapse prevention strategies and a written plan outlining coping strategies
  • establish an action plan to achieve 3 personal development goals, like employment or education

Session details

The programme is made up of 1 to 9 one-to-one sessions, depending on participants' needs.
