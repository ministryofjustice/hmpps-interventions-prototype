North Yorkshire: Dependency and Recovery activities

Activity sessions are available to any adult men on probation in the following locations:

  • Harrogate
  • Northallerton
  • Scarborough
  • Skipton
  • York

One-to-one or group sessions

The following activities are available as a one-to-one or group session:

  • Addressing harmful or negative relationships
  • Alcohol Brief intervention
  • Arts activities
  • Departure Lounges (where available)
  • Digital Inclusion
  • Diversion
  • Drinking and Me
  • Health Trainer or Health Champion support
  • Hope in Recovery
  • Ingeus Academy
  • Managing-Me
  • Map of Me
  • Me and My Emotions
  • Sports activities
  • Trigger Rehearsal
  • Understanding cravings
  • Geese Theatre: “USED” (group session only)

One-to-one sessions

The following activities are available as one-to-one sessions:

  • ABEI
  • Breaking Free Online
  • Co-ordinate
  • Drink Coach
  • Engage with other Probation service providers
  • Getting To Know You
  • Guided Recovery Journaling
  • Inside Out
  • Keeping Me Safe
  • Link-it
  • Make Time Count
  • Mutual aid
  • My Choices
  • Navigate from the Gate
  • Out-of-hours service for crisis-management
  • Pathways to Change
  • Plotting the Course
  • Re-Engage
  • Satnav

Group sessions

The activity Geese Theatre: “USED” is offered as a group session.
