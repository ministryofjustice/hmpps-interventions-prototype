Overview (Intervention summary)

Aims to support desistance from Intimate Partner Violence (IPV) offending by enabling participants to overcome challenges such as: relationship problems; social skills deficits; attitudes that support relationship violence; aggression and anger; and, emotional mismanagement / self-regulation.

Who is eligible

You can refer for this intervention if all of the following apply:

  • they are available for all offence types
  • they are both male and female
  • they are aged 18 or over
  • If no SARA or Medium+ on SARA consider BBR
  • AND If case is not suitable for BBR, then OVP: 20+ (If no OVP, 25+ OGRS)
