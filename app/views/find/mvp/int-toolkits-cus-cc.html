Description

Choices and Changes is a toolkit of exercises for prison staff working with young adults with low maturity. This can include trouble making decisions, managing emotions or understanding consequences.

The resources can also support men aged 25 to 30 with similar needs.

Session details

There are 6 topics in the toolkit. Each 30-minute topic focuses on a different need.
