Who is eligible

You can refer for this intervention if all of the following apply:

  • they are available for all offence types
  • they are in community or custody setting
  • they are both male and female
  • they are aged 18 or over
  • they have static risk level of medium and above
  • they have OGRS3 score of 50 or above
  • Treatment needs (risk factors) targeted by the programme, assessed through OASys using specified items, or the standalone TSP Needs Assessment Tool when an OASys is not available
