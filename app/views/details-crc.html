Thinking Skills

TSP is a cognitive skills programme for men and women designed to help develop skills in pro-social problem solving, perspective taking, developing and managing relationships, and self-management, and encourages pro-social attitudes, behaviour and goals for the future. TSP has been designed to incorporate maximum responsivity and flexibility of delivery format.

REASONABLE ADJUSTMENTS: should be made to support individuals with reading and writing difficulties through the programme

LEARNING DISABILITY: Potential individuals should have the intellectual and social functioning ability to engage with the material. If appropriate, individuals should be referred to New Me Sdivengths (NMS) which has been designed to meet the needs of people with learning disabilities and challenges.

MENTAL ILLNESS: For some potential individuals with identified mental illness, or personality disorder it might not be the right time for them to participate.

LENGTH NEEDED: Potential individuals should have enough time on their sentence or Order to complete all programme sessions and the post programme review.

SEX OFFENDERS: For men convicted of sexual offences the HMPPS sdivategy is that they should be considered for Horizon (offence specific) and Kaizen in the FIRST INSTANCE in accordance with RNR principles as defined for those programmes.

DOMESTIC VIOLENCE OFFENDERS: DV offenders are allowed, but not IPV.
